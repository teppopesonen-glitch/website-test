Welcome

Welcome to my personal site where I document my passion for mountain climbing, adventure travel, and exploration. Join me as I share stories from my hikes, photos from my climbs, and lessons learned from life above the clouds.

About Me

Hi, I’m a 37-year-old adventurer whose journey began in 2014 when I first tried indoor climbing. What started as a fun challenge quickly became a lifelong passion — one that grew alongside my love for travel. Over the years, my curiosity for new experiences evolved into a drive for bigger adventures, eventually leading me from climbing walls to real mountains around the world.

Each year, I strive to push my limits a little further — improving my skills, resilience, and connection with nature. My goal isn’t just to reach higher peaks, but to become a better version of myself through every climb and every journey.

Latest Adventures

Mount Kazbek – Georgia

July 2025

A challenging but deeply rewarding expedition in the Caucasus Mountains. The landscape was raw and vast, and the teamwork it demanded made this climb stand out as one of my all-time favorites.

Storsteinsfjellet – Norway

July 2024

A scenic climb above the fjords near Tromsø. The combination of northern lights, snow-covered ridges, and Arctic silence made this one of my most peaceful adventures yet.

Cotopaxi – Ecuador

November 2022

An active volcano and one of Ecuador’s most iconic peaks. The climb combined technical ice sections and stunning views of the clouds below. It was both a mental and physical test I’ll never forget.

Santa Cruz Hike – Peru

October 2022

A four-day trek through the Cordillera Blanca with glacial lakes, Andean villages, and endless mountain views. This hike reminded me how powerful and peaceful the Peruvian wilderness can be.

Huayna Potosi – Bolivia

September 2022

At 6,088 meters, Huayna Potosi was my first true high-altitude challenge in the Andes. The sunrise from the summit was unforgettable — a breathtaking reward after a long night of climbing ice and snow under the stars.
